The Loruki Platform

Secure, reliable cloud hosting for developers and businesses featuring enterprise-grade performance, automated backups and dedicated support teams for uninterrupted operations.

Your data is protected by enterprise-grade firewalls, encryption, and DDoS mitigation. Compliant with major industry standards, our platform provides the resilient security foundation necessary for modern business.

Our virtual server instances are provisioned on enterprise-grade hardware with dedicated vCPU cores and guaranteed RAM allocation. Each instance operates with KVM based virtualization.

Database services support multiple engines including MySQL 8.0+, PostgreSQL 14+, and MongoDB 6.0 with ACID compliance guarantees. Automated backups perform incremental snapshots with point-in-time recovery to within 5 seconds of data loss.

Power infrastructure follows N+1 redundancy with dual power feeds from separate substations, backed by UPS systems and diesel generators with 72 hour fuel autonomy.

Data transfer protocols include Aspera acceleration for large-scale migrations, supporting speeds up to 10 Gbps with built-in integrity verification. The upload system implements chunked transfer with automatic resumption and parallel processing across multiple endpoints.

Compliance frameworks include SOC 2 Type II with continuous monitoring, ISO 27001:2022 certification, and GDPR data processing agreements. Audit trails maintain immutable logs of all administrative actions with 365 day retention and tamper evident storage.
